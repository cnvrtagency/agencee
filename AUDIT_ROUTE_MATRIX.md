## Remaining Exceptions

- `/api/auth/google/callback` is reached by Google without app auth headers. It verifies signed, expiring OAuth state generated by authenticated `/api/auth/google`.
- `/api/schedule/check` is reached by Vercel cron. It uses Vercel cron signature or `CRON_SECRET` and fails closed when `CRON_SECRET` is missing.
